    About Hidden Treasures

    Hidden Treasures is a website that is trying to help support and motivate teenagers and young adults when they may be faced with a time of difficulty. This website is a positive place for people to go when they need support and motivation. This site is a stigma free zone around mental health. Many university students are faced with mental health issues and they don’t necessarily have somewhere to turn when needed or somewhere health to turn.

    Students with poor mental well-being have continued to arise. Although there are many mental health counselling services, there is still a negative stigma against mental health. Either students are given poor coping strategies or they are not going to get the help that they need. Hidden Treasure believes that this is a way to avoid the negative stigma and can be beneficial to the students.
