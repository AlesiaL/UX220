About Hidden Treasures

Hidden Treasures is a website that is trying to help support and motivate teenagers and young adults when they may be faced with a time of difficulty. This website is a positive place for people to go when they need support and motivation. This site is a stigma free zone around mental health. Our Mission.

Students with poor mental well-being have continued to arise. Although there are many mental health counselling services, there is still a negative stigma against mental health. Either students are given poor coping strategies or they are not going to get the help that they need. Hidden Treasure believes that this is a way to avoid the negative stigma and can be beneficial to the students.

Our Core Values.

We want to create a stigma free environment in which people could feel comfortable and supported.
